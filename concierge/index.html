About Brush Concierge

Our Story

Brush Concierge was founded by Emma Chen, a professional makeup artist with over 15 years of experience working with celebrities and major beauty brands. After witnessing countless clients struggle with breakouts caused by improperly cleaned brushes, Emma developed a proprietary cleaning system that maintains brush integrity while removing all buildup and residue.

What started as a service for her professional network quickly grew into Brush Concierge, the premier brush care service trusted by makeup artists, beauty enthusiasts, and celebrities alike.
