Brush Concierge provides professional makeup brush cleaning services through a subscription-based model. Our service includes:

  • Quality assurance and inspection
  • Customer support and account management

2.1 Billing Cycle

Subscriptions are billed monthly on the date you first subscribe. Your subscription will automatically renew each month unless you cancel.

Prices are subject to change with 30 days notice to subscribers.

2.3 Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting customer support. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
