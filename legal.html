Terms of Service

Please read these terms carefully before using our service

1. Service Description

Brush Concierge provides professional makeup brush cleaning services through a monthly subscription model. Our service includes:

  • Professional cleaning of up to 20 makeup brushes per monthly subscription cycle
  • Free shipping both ways within the United States
  • 5-7 business day turnaround time from receipt to return shipment
  • Use of professional-grade cleaning solutions and sanitization processes
  • Quality inspection and basic brush maintenance

Service Limitations

Our service is limited to makeup brushes only. We do not clean sponges, beauty blenders, or other makeup tools. Brushes must be in reasonable condition and suitable for professional cleaning.

1.2 Service Availability and Limitations

As we are currently validating our service model:

  • Service availability may be limited or temporarily suspended
  • Processing times may vary during high-demand periods
  • We reserve the right to limit the number of new subscribers

We will communicate any service changes or limitations via email and our website.

2. Subscription Terms and Billing

2.1 Subscription Fee

The monthly subscription fee is $19.99, charged automatically on your subscription anniversary date each month. Payment is processed securely through Stripe, our trusted payment processor.

2.2 Automatic Renewal

Your subscription will automatically renew each month unless cancelled at least 24 hours before your next billing date. You will be charged the then-current subscription fee for each renewal period.

2.3 Payment Methods

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods processed through Stripe. You are responsible for maintaining current and valid payment information in your account.

2.4 Failed Payments

If a payment fails, we will attempt to process payment up to 3 times over a 7-day period. If payment continues to fail, your subscription may be suspended until payment is successful.

3. Shipping and Handling

3.1 Shipping Process

We provide prepaid shipping labels for sending your brushes to our facility and returning them to you. You are responsible for securely packaging your brushes using the materials and instructions provided.

3.2 Turnaround Time

Our standard turnaround time is 5-7 business days from when we receive your brushes to when we ship them back. This timeframe may be extended during peak periods, holidays, or unforeseen circumstances.

3.3 Shipping Restrictions

Our service is currently available only within the United States. We ship to all 50 states but cannot ship to P.O. boxes for return shipments.

3.4 Shipping Insurance and Liability

We provide shipping insurance coverage for your brushes during transit:

IMPORTANT: We are not responsible for loss or damage that occurs:

  • During postal/shipping carrier transit (covered by shipping insurance only)

For brushes valued over $500 total, customers may purchase additional insurance through the shipping carrier at their own expense. We recommend customers photograph their brushes before shipping and declare accurate values.
